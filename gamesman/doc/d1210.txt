[Description]
	1,2,....10 is a simple puzzle game that involves moving a counter one or two spaces. 1,2,....10 has several variants, including the 1,2,...4 and Henry Ernest Dudeney's 37 Puzzle Game.

[Rules]
Rules:

The number starts out at one and the players take turns increasing it by either one or two. Eventually, one player will be within two of 10 and they will be able to increase the number to 10, winning the game.

To move: The first player may either move one or two spaces on his or her turn.

To win: To be the first player to raise the total above ten.

To win misere: To force your opponent into raising the total above ten.
