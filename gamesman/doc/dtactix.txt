Rules:

TacTix is a two-player game in which the players take turns removing pieces from the board. On a player's turn, he/she can remove adjacent pieces from a single row or column of the board. The pieces must be touching in order to be removed together. The player who is forced to make the last move loses.

To move: Select either a line/circle that you wish to remove

To win: To remove the last stone on the board.

To win misere: To force your opponent to remove the last piece from the board.
