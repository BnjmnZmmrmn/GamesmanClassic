Rules:

Also known as Honeycomb, Sim can be finished in less than 15 moves. The game starts out with six dots. Players take turns connecting 2 of these dots with a line. A line cannot be drawn twice and lines of the same color cannot form a triangle of if its end points are part of the outer dots.

To move: The first player may either move one or two spaces on his or her turn.

To win: Force your opponent to complete a triangle.

To win misere: To complete a triangle before your opponent.
